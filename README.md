1. **Start a New Project**: Open transcribee and create a new project.
2. **Upload Your Video**: Click on the "Upload" button to import your video file.
3. **Begin Transcription**: Select the "Transcribe" option. The application will start processing your video.
4. **Review and Edit**: Once the transcription is complete, review the text. You can make edits directly in the application.
5. **Organize Your Text**: Use the features to categorize and save your transcriptions in a structured format.

## üôã Frequently Asked Questions

### How long does it take to transcribe a video?

The transcription time depends on the length of the video. Typically, it takes approximately one minute per minute of video.

### Can I use transcribee for different languages?

Currently, transcribee supports English, with plans to include other languages in future updates.

### What type of video files do I need to use?

You can use most common video formats, including `.mp4`, `.mov`, and `.avi`.
